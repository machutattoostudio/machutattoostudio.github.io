About the Artist

Tattoo artist Suresh Machu, He has completed his Computer science engineering in Anna university and MBA in UOM, Bangalore. He is Passionate about Tattoo from his childhood. At this point he decided to learn tattooing Professionally.He learnt tattooing in the year 2009 under the guidance of Kevin Andrade and advance certification course in Thailand. Initially Suresh Machu started tattooing from home on a Interest basis. Slowly he established a good reputation and the demand for his tattoos grew. He decided to start tattooing full time. He started Machu Tattoo Studio in Bangalore & Madurai has been running it successfully since. He has trained several artists during this period, most of whom are now running other studios successfully. He specializes in all types of art forms from Portraits to simple kanji characters. He has done over 1200 tattoos during this period. His work has been appreciated by all clients.
